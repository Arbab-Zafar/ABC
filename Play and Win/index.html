Guess and Win

About the game :-

The game will generate a random number.

You have to guess the number in the given chances (the chances are given randomly) given according to the amount paid.

If you guess the number in the given chances then you will win.

Note:If "You are close" displays then the random number is within the range of 3

The price, chances and winning amount:-

Give 1 rupee and get 4, 5 or 6 chances. If won - 2 rupees!

Give 2 rupees and get 4, 5 or 6 chances. If won - 3 rupees!

Give 3 rupees and get 4, 5 or 6 chances. If won - 4 rupees!

Give 4 rupees and get 5 chances. If won - 6 rupees!

Give 5 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 8 rupees!

Give 6 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 10 rupees!

Give 7 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 11 rupees!

Give 8 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 12 rupees!

Give 9 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 14 rupees!

Give 10 rupees and get 4 or 5 chances. If won - 17 rupees!

Calculate and Win

About the game :-

To play this game you have to give 5 rupees.

The game will give you some random calculations to do!

The game has three levels. If you pass them then you will win.

Note: In this game remainder and decimals are not counted!

The time and winning amount:-

If you pass the first level and got out then you will win 1 rupee!

If you pass the second level and got out then you will win 5 rupees!

If you pass all the three levels then you will win 10 rupees!

Unscramble and Win

About the game :-

To play this game you have to give 5 rupees.

The game will generate an scrambled meaningful word!

If you guessed the word then you will win.

Note:You must have to write the first letter of the word in capital.

The time, chances and winning amount:-

You will get 20 seconds to guess the word.

You will 3 chances to guess the word!

If you win then you will get 7 rupees!
